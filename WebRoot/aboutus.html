Our Story

  • company's history

Our company is a smart special robot enterprise integrating light, machine, electricity and control technology. Through learning, inheritance, transformation and innovation, we provide revolutionary intelligent special operation products and solutions with high reliability and high performance for intelligent special robot industry, industrial users and high-risk special operations. Based on intelligent special robot products, we focus on intelligent navigation controllers and multi-machine collaborative scheduling systems independently developed by the team to deepen the combination of production, study and research, and to customize application development and system integration for users in different industries. The technical team is stationed in the industry application site, actively explores the demand, strives to polish the products, continues to serve the customers, strives for excellence, and creates a reliable, easy-to-use and practical intelligent special robot product with ingenuity. CRETOCE is customer-oriented, strives for the future, and continues to innovate. With the mission of “Everyone can be an explorer”, CRETOCE is eager to exchange and cooperate with experts from various industries.
