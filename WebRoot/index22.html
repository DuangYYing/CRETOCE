How Can Our Robot Help You?

Exploring the unknown area

Explorers can control the car remotely via Bluetooth, and monitor the status of the car in real time, collecting the luminosity, temperature and humidity data of the environment around the car, so as to realize the remote monitoring function of the harsh environment .Thus the explorer can arrange the adventure more reasonably and safely.

Follow your footsteps

The outdoor adventure Z1 car will automatically follow the explorer and feedback the environmental data around the explorer to the explorer's mobile phone in real time. Therefore, explorers can carry it easily and save space.

Perfect obstacle avoidance

Outdoor adventures may encounter harsh environments. The car can be safely protected by infrared obstacle avoidance. The explorer does not need to worry about the safety of the car. Therefore, the outdoor adventure Z1 car can replace the explorer to the unknown area to collect data such as environment and image.
